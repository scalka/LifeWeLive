DANCEHALL

Music genre originated in the late 70s from reggae. The dancehall, as a dance, concentrates above all on the moves. It is in Jamaica where such a unique connection of dance and music is found. There are songs about just one dance move, on the other hand you have songs in which you can hear several dance moves.
