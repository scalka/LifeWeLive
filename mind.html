Take time to plan and prioritize.
The most common source of stress is the perception that you've got too much work to do. Rather than obsess about it, pick one thing that, if you get it done today, will move you closer to your highest goal and purpose in life.

Develop your own work flow which could include reading breaks and walks. The goal is to work for about 90 min without interruptions and then have a short break, around 15-20 min, maybe read or go for a short walk. By doing this you will come back refreshed and with a clear mind, ready for your next challenge.
